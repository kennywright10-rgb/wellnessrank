Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Thrive 4 Peak Performance ("we," "us," or "our") collects information to provide medical and wellness services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, date of birth, contact information, address
  • Health Information: Medical history, current medications, treatment records (protected under HIPAA)
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address (processed securely)
  • Website Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via cookies)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide medical treatments and wellness services
  • Process appointments and payments
  • Communicate about your treatment plans
  • Send appointment reminders and health information (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. HIPAA Compliance

Your health information is protected under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all medical records
  • Share health information only with your consent or as required by law
  • Train all staff on HIPAA compliance and privacy procedures
  • Use secure systems to store and transmit health data

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care (with your authorization)
  • Insurance companies for billing purposes (if applicable)
  • Service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Law enforcement or regulatory agencies when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on data protection

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your medical records
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures
  • Request restrictions on use of your information
  • File a complaint if you believe your privacy has been violated

7. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some functionality.

8. Email and Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we use your email address to send wellness tips and promotional offers. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date indicates when changes were made. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
