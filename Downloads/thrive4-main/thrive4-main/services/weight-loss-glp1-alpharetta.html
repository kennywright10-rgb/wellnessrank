What Is GLP-1 Weight Loss Therapy?

GLP-1 (Glucagon-Like Peptide-1) agonists like semaglutide and tirzepatide represent a paradigm shift in medically supervised weight loss. Rather than relying on willpower and restriction, GLP-1 medications work biologically by mimicking your natural appetite-regulating hormone. They slow stomach emptying, increase satiety signals to your brain, reduce hunger hormones, and improve insulin sensitivity—creating a biological state where eating less happens naturally, not through deprivation. The result: meaningful weight loss (15-25% of body weight) maintained over time without constant hunger or restriction.

What makes GLP-1 different from traditional weight loss approaches is the mechanism of action. You're not suppressing appetite through stimulants or blocking nutrients—you're creating a more efficient metabolic state where your body naturally gravitates toward appropriate caloric intake. Combined with our lifestyle coaching focused on nutrition, exercise, and sustainable habits, GLP-1 therapy produces results comparable to surgical interventions but without surgery.   • Anyone ready for lifestyle change — Most effective when combined with improved nutrition and exercise habits

Contraindications & Cautions

  • Personal or family history of thyroid cancer — GLP-1 agonists contraindicated due to theoretical thyroid risk
  • Severe kidney disease — GLP-1 can worsen kidney function; requires physician assessment
  • History of pancreatitis — GLP-1 associated with pancreatitis risk; careful physician evaluation needed
  • Pregnancy or planning pregnancy — GLP-1 medications require discontinuation; consult your OB-GYN
  • Severe diabetic retinopathy — Rapid weight loss can worsen eye complications; requires monitoring
  • Certain other medications — Some drugs interact; full medication review required before starting

Frequently Asked Questions

How do GLP-1 medications work for weight loss?

GLP-1 (Glucagon-Like Peptide-1) agonists like semaglutide and tirzepatide work by mimicking your natural GLP-1 hormone. They slow stomach emptying, increase feelings of fullness, reduce hunger signals to the brain, and improve insulin sensitivity. This biological approach to appetite control means you eat less naturally without willpower, while also improving blood sugar regulation.

What is the difference between semaglutide and tirzepatide?

Semaglutide targets GLP-1 receptors only. Tirzepatide targets both GLP-1 and GIP receptors, providing dual hormone mimicry. Tirzepatide typically produces 20-25% weight loss while semaglutide produces 15-20% weight loss. Both are effective; your physician will recommend based on your specific needs and medical history.

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Average weight loss with semaglutide is 15-20% of body weight. With tirzepatide, results average 20-25% of body weight. Actual results depend on your starting weight, adherence to the protocol, exercise, and nutrition habits. A 200-pound person might lose 40-50 pounds (semaglutide) or 50-60 pounds (tirzepatide) over 6-12 months.

How long does it take to see results from GLP-1 therapy?

You may notice reduced appetite within the first week or two. Visible weight loss typically becomes apparent within 2-4 weeks as your body enters a caloric deficit naturally. Significant results (10+ pounds) are usually apparent by 8-12 weeks. Maximum results develop over 6-12 months of consistent therapy.

Are GLP-1 medications safe?

GLP-1 agonists are FDA-approved and have extensive safety data. Common side effects are mild and usually temporary: nausea, constipation, or diarrhea that resolve as your body adjusts. Serious side effects are rare. Our physicians monitor your response through regular check-ins and blood work to ensure safety throughout your program.

What happens if I stop taking GLP-1 medication?

When you stop GLP-1 therapy, appetite signals gradually return to normal over weeks. Weight regain can occur if you don't maintain the habits you've developed (nutrition, exercise). Many people transition to maintenance doses to preserve results. Our program includes lifestyle coaching to help you maintain weight loss long-term.
