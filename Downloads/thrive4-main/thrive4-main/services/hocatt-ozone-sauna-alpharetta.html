Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a HOCATT™ session?

Each session lasts 25-30 minutes. We recommend allowing 45-60 minutes total including check-in, changing, and cool-down time.

Is HOCATT™ safe?

Yes, when performed under medical supervision. The HOCATT™ chamber is FDA-approved and uses established therapeutic modalities. Our board-certified physicians screen all patients before their first session. The dual destructor system ensures no ozone escapes into the room.

How often should I do HOCATT™ therapy?

Most patients start with 2-3 sessions per week for 4-6 weeks to build therapeutic levels. After initial protocol, many transition to weekly or bi-weekly maintenance sessions. Your physician will create a personalized protocol based on your health goals.

What should I wear?

Most patients wear a swimsuit or minimal clothing (underwear is fine). You'll sit in the chamber with your head outside, so there's complete privacy. We provide towels and a private changing area. You can shower after your session if desired.

Are there side effects?

Most people feel relaxed and energized. Some may experience mild detox symptoms (headache, fatigue, muscle aches) in the first 24-48 hours, which indicates the body is releasing toxins. These typically resolve quickly. Staying well-hydrated minimizes any detox symptoms.

Can I combine HOCATT™ with other therapies?

Absolutely! HOCATT™ pairs exceptionally well with IV therapy (especially NAD+ or glutathione), red light therapy, and compression therapy. Many patients do IV therapy immediately before or after HOCATT™ for enhanced detoxification and cellular support.
