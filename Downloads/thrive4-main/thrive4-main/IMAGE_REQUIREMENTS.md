## IMAGE OPTIMIZATION GUIDELINES

### File Formats
- **Photos**: Use `.jpg` (compress to 80-85% quality)
- **Graphics/Icons**: Use `.svg` when possible, `.png` with transparency
- **Logo**: Use `.svg` for scalability, `.png` fallback

### Compression
- Hero images: Max 300KB each
- Service cards: Max 150KB each
- Team photos: Max 200KB each
- Icons: Max 20KB each

### Naming Convention
- Use lowercase
- Use hyphens (not underscores or spaces)
- Be descriptive: `service-iv-therapy.jpg` not `img-001.jpg`

### Alt Text Requirements
All images need descriptive alt text for SEO and accessibility. Examples:

- `hero-home.jpg` → "Modern wellness clinic interior with natural lighting at Thrive 4 Peak Performance in Alpharetta, GA"
- `service-iv-therapy.jpg` → "IV therapy vitamin infusion bag being prepared by medical professional"
- `team-doctor-1.jpg` → "Dr. [Name], board-certified physician at Thrive 4 Peak Performance"

## PHOTOGRAPHY BRIEF FOR VENDOR

If hiring a photographer, provide this brief:

**Style**: Clean, modern medical aesthetic. NOT stock photography look. Authentic, natural lighting, minimal editing.

**Subjects**: Real medical staff in professional attire, real patients (with releases), actual clinic equipment.

**Lighting**: Soft natural light preferred. No harsh shadows. Warm, inviting tones while maintaining clinical credibility.

**Composition**: Mix of wide shots (show environment) and medium shots (show people/equipment). Avoid extreme close-ups.

**Mood**: Premium, calm, trustworthy, approachable. Not cold or overly clinical. Not spa-like or too relaxed.

**Avoid**: Stock photo aesthetics, overly posed shots, fake patients, excessive Photoshop, dramatic lighting effects.
